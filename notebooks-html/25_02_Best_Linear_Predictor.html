Best Linear Predictor¶

A Linear Predictor¶

$$ \hat{Y}_\mathbf{b} ~ = ~ \mathbf{b}^T(\mathbf{X} - \mathbf{\mu}_\mathbf{X}) + \mu_Y $$

where $$ \mathbf{b} ~ = ~ \boldsymbol{\Sigma}_\mathbf{X}^{-1} \boldsymbol{\Sigma}_{\mathbf{X}, Y} $$

Projection¶

$$ W ~ = ~ Y - \hat{Y}_\mathbf{b} $$\begin{align*} Cov(W, \mathbf{a}^T\mathbf{X}) ~ &= ~ Cov(Y - \hat{Y}_\mathbf{b}, \mathbf{a}^T\mathbf{X}) \\ &= ~ Cov(Y, \mathbf{a}^T\mathbf{X}) - Cov(\hat{Y}_\mathbf{b}, \mathbf{a}^T\mathbf{X}) \\ &= ~ Cov(Y, \mathbf{a}^T\mathbf{X}) - Cov(\mathbf{b}^T\mathbf{X}, \mathbf{a}^T\mathbf{X}) \\ &= ~ \mathbf{a}^T\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_{\mathbf{X}, Y} - \mathbf{a}^T\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_\mathbf{X} \mathbf{b} \\ &= ~ \mathbf{a}^T\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_{\mathbf{X}, Y} - \mathbf{a}^T\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_\mathbf{X} \boldsymbol{\Sigma}_\mathbf{X}^{-1}\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_{\mathbf{X}, Y} \\ &= ~ 0 \end{align*}
